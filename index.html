How Does It Work?

  1. You send us a request, and we organise a free survey (It can be done remotely if that is convenient for you). If you want you can use our house moving calculator to estimate your price and send your query via its quote form.
  2. Our moving experts will discuss with you all the options you have in regards to the packing and transportation of the goods.
  3. Once all is clear, together with you, we will set up a moving date.
  4. Our crew will come to your UK home, pack or get your already packed boxes and items and load them to the vehicles.
  5. We will safely transport your home items to their new location.
  6. If you want after the unloading, we can also unpack everything for you.
